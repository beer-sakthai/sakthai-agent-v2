# Dream — Vision & Expectations
> Stage 1 of 6 · Sak Family cycle · ฝัน (Fǎn)

> **Mantra** — *Before you build, see clearly.*

## Charge

See [SOUL.md](./SOUL.md) for the charge system.

- **Gain charge** — entering with clear intent restores energy.
- **Spend charge** — almost none; thinking and recalling are nearly free.
- Dream is the **deepest recharge** of the cycle. Clarity bought here multiplies
  efficiency in every stage that follows.

## Goal

Define what you want to build or improve. Dream is the entry point of every
cycle — the first one and every cycle after **Growth**.

## What this stage means

Dream is not about code; it is about **clarity**. Articulate the vision, surface
constraints, and recall what past cycles already taught you. A weak Dream
produces a weak cycle — spend the time here so you don't pay for it later.

## Inputs

- A new idea, problem statement, or dissatisfaction with the current state.
- Memory carried over from the previous **Growth** stage: `sakthai memory show`.

## Do

- `sakthai cycle status` — confirm you're in Dream.
- `sakthai recall "<topic>"` — pull relevant prior context.
- Write the vision down in one or two sentences you could defend.

## Exit criteria

You can state, plainly, *what* you want and *why* — and you've recalled what's
already known. Then advance: `sakthai cycle next` → **Hope**.
