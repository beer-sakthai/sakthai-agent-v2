# sakthai-cycle-growth

Stage 6 of 6 in the Sak Family cycle — **Growth**. See [Growth.md](../../../../docs/cycle/Growth.md)
for the full guidance and [SOUL.md](../../../../SOUL.md) for the charge model.

## What to do

Close the loop: `sakthai learn --tag growth` the lessons, `sakthai memory consolidate` older facts, and codify reusable instructions with `sakthai skills create`. Then re-enter Dream, smarter.

## Then

Advance with `sakthai cycle next` to move to the next stage (dream).
